# Code Of Conduct

This project follows the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while being clear about technical concerns.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the model, documentation, tooling, or project quality.
- Accept feedback gracefully and help others do the same.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Any conduct that maintainers reasonably judge inappropriate in a professional open-source project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation when needed to protect the project community.

Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported through the maintainers or the Elan8 organization contact channels.
